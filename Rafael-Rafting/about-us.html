Rafael Rafting – More Than a Ride, It’s a Passion

Our Story: Born for Adventure, Driven by the River!

Rafael Rafting is a family-owned business that has been providing thrilling whitewater rafting experiences for over 20 years. Our team of expert guides is dedicated to ensuring that you have a safe and unforgettable adventure on the Nile. We offer a range of trips for all skill levels, from beginner-friendly excursions to challenging expert runs.

At Rafael Rafting, safety is our top priority. Our guides are certified professionals with years of experience navigating the Nile's rapids. We provide all the necessary equipment, including life jackets and helmets, and conduct thorough safety briefings before each trip. You can relax and enjoy the ride, knowing that you are in good hands.

Whether you're looking for an adrenaline-pumping adventure or a leisurely float down the river, Rafael Rafting has the perfect trip for you. Join us for an unforgettable experience on the Nile and discover the thrill of whitewater rafting!

Safety Guidelines & Equipment

  • All guests are required to wear life jackets at all times.
  • Guests must be physically fit to participate in rafting trips.
  • All equipment, including helmets and paddles, is provided by us and must be used according to guide instructions.
  • No alcohol is allowed before or during the trip for safety reasons.
  • Guides conduct a safety briefing before every trip to ensure everyone is informed.
