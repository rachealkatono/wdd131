Rafael Rafting Adventure

Site Name

Rafael Rafting Adventure

This name reflects the company's adventurous spirit and focus on whitewater rafting. The alliteration makes it catchy, and "Adventure" conveys excitement. Suggested domain: https://rachealkatono.github.io/wdd131/project/siteplan.html

Site Purpose

The website serves as a comprehensive resource for adventure seekers by:

  • Providing details on whitewater rafting packages and safety guidelines
  • Allowing easy online booking and inquiries
  • Sharing testimonials and adventure stories from past visitors
  • Educating visitors on environmental conservation efforts

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "What safety measures are in place for first-time rafters?"

Scenario 2: "What are the best rafting trips for families with young children?"

Color Schema

Accent Color: #06B6D4 (Highlights and navigation elements)

Background Color: #F8FAFC (General background, cards, and sections)

Typography

Montserrat: Used for headers and navigation
